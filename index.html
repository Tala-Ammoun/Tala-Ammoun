About Me

Tala Ammoun is a descendant of the ancient Egyptian deity Amun, the Zeus of Egypt; the god of air who later merged with the god of sun to become Amun-Ra. Tala hopes to set up her own business and conquer the world some day.

    Achievements:
  • 1st Class Honors Degree in (BA) International Relations from Richmond University in August 2020.
  • Hosted a podcast called "Review Your Rights" for the Freedom Law Clinic in May 2021.
  • Was a speaker at TEDx RAIUL in March 2019, the title of the talk was “Modernity makes us more psychologically complex”.
  • Performed stand-up comedy shows at Up the Creek Comedy Club and Angel Comedy Club in October and November 2021 respectively.
